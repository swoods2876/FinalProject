Joel Foster is an artist living and working in Wassaic, New York. Six year ago Joel was diagnosed with a disease known as Stargardt's Disease. The progressive vision loss associated with Stargardt disease is caused by the death of photoreceptor cells in the central portion of the retina. Despite losing most of his central vision Joel continues to work as a visual artist.
